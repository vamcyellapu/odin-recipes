Chicken Parmesan Recipe

Description

This Chicken Parmesan recipe delivers a crispy breaded chicken breast topped with melted mozzarella and rich tomato sauce. It is a comforting Italian-inspired dish that feels special while staying simple enough for home cooking.

The chicken is coated in seasoned breadcrumbs and baked until golden, then finished with marinara and cheese before a final broil. Serve it with pasta or a fresh side salad for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning
  • Olive oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Pound the chicken breasts to an even thickness.
  3. Coat each breast in flour, dip in beaten egg, then press into the breadcrumb mixture.
  4. Cook the chicken in a hot skillet with olive oil until golden on both sides.
  5. Place chicken in a baking dish, spoon marinara sauce over each piece, and top with mozzarella slices.
  6. Bake for 15–20 minutes until the cheese melts and the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Serve hot with extra sauce and pasta or vegetables.
