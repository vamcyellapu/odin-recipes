Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe brings the classic Italian comfort food experience to your kitchen with layers of tender pasta, savory meat sauce, and creamy cheese. The finished dish is rich, hearty, and perfect for family dinners or any special meal where you want a satisfying home-cooked dish.

The recipe includes a simple meat sauce made from beef, sausage, onion, garlic, and tomato sauce, plus a creamy ricotta and mozzarella cheese mixture. It builds flavor in every layer and bakes to a golden, bubbly finish.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 ounces tomato pasta sauce
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  3. Cook onion and garlic in a skillet, then add ground beef and sausage. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer until slightly thickened.
  5. Mix ricotta, half of the mozzarella, Parmesan, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning in a bowl.
  6. Layer sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  7. Repeat layers and finish with remaining mozzarella on top.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes, then let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
