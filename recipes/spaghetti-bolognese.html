Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Description

This classic Spaghetti Bolognese recipe combines tender pasta with a rich tomato and meat sauce for a comforting dinner that is easy to make. The sauce simmers slowly with onion, garlic, carrots, and herbs, creating a deep flavor that coats every strand of spaghetti.

Perfect for weeknight meals or family gatherings, this version uses simple ingredients and builds layers of savory taste with a touch of red wine and Italian seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package directions.
  2. Sauté onion, garlic, and carrot in olive oil until softened.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and red wine, then add oregano and basil.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 20–25 minutes until thickened.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then serve over spaghetti.
  7. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
